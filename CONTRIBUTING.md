# Policy

## AI

AI tools and automation are used by the project maintainer for development, testing, and operational tasks - but always with careful manual human review to ensure outputs are not low-quality, inaccurate, vulnerable, non-compliant with project standards, or just plain slop. AI-generated issues or contributing code, documentation, or other content must also abide by this policy before submission: Improper or careless use of AI tools will be closed/removed and ignored.

TLDR: I don't have a problem with AI. I do have a problem with using "ai" as an excuse for slop/low quality.
